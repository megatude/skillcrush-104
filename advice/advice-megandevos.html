Hi my name is Megan and I have some advice for you!

Work on your classes EVERY DAY!

If you get behind and can't work on it a little bit every day you will lose your progress and it will make things harder.

Even if you're just reviewing content or practicing make sure you do this every day!

Type out the problem you're having.

I can't tell you how many times I figured out the answer to my problem by writing out my question for the google group.
